about me

what do i have to say about me... i'm just a normal guy with lots of hobbies...

since my childhood i was interested in technologies and all my remote controlled cars are the proof of it: after some time i'd open them up and try to understand how it worked and, in most of the cases, take some of the pieces of it until it didn't work. but once i made one damaged radio play again (don't ask me how...)

in my 12th year i had to choose which course i'd take so i chose informatics engineering because of the interest i had in that area. at that time i had to choose the faculty i'd study. i chose isep, as the advice of teachers and family members, but i didn't like it, so, after one year, i changed to feup and i don't regret my choice

once here, i never looked back and i successfully completed my master degree in five years

i spent my last year of studies abroad under the erasmus program. i was in zagreb, croatia. this experience helped me improve my english skills and tested my ability to live in a completely different environment, with a different culture. it also taught me some croatian skills

as from now, i'm trying to find a job in one of my areas of interest, preferably one that requires me to keep growing in several of these areas

throughout my studies i crossed paths with several fields of studies and, from those, i developed a special affection for human-computer interaction, mobile computing, web applications and user interfaces design and usability
